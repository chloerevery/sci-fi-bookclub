Classic Sci-Fi Short Stories

A Reading List for Jenkins Book Club


  1. E.M. Forster, "The Machine Stops" (1909)

    2. -A prescient story, given that it was written over a century ago. Longish.

  2. Isaac Asimov, "Nightfall" (1941)

    3. -Medium length.

  3. Harlan Elison, "I Have No Mouth, And I Must Scream" (1967)

    4. -A sci-fi/horror story. Longish.

  4. Kurt Vonnegut, Jr. "Harrison Bergeron" (1961)

    5. -One of the short stories that made Vonnegut famous. Medium length.

  5. Arthur C. Clarke, "The Nine Billion Names of God" (1953)

    6. -Very short.

  6. Frederik Pohl, "The Tunnel Under The World" (1955)

    7. -A riveting story. Longish.

  7. Isaac Asimov, "The Last Question" (1956)

    8. -Probably the most famous story on this list. Short.
